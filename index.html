Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about Fethr.

Is Fethr only for hiking?

Fethr is built to support any and all kinds of outdoor trips—camping, climbing, skiing, bikepacking, day hikes, you name it!

Can I use Fethr without internet access?

Yes! All features of the app work without any internet access.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required! Everything is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any personal information.

What do I get with Fethr Pro?

Fethr Pro removes some restrictions by allowing unlimited packs and photos per trip, data import/export, and more!

Can I track the total weight of my gear?

Yes! Fethr automatically calculates pack weight based on the items you add.

How is Fethr different from other gear/pack tools?

Other commonly used tools are web based and Fethr is a native app for mobile devices. Fethr also has more than just gear management such as trip planning and route tracking.
